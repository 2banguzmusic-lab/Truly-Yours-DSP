Our Mission

Truly Yours DSP was created to put power back into the hands of artists.

Artists keep 100% ownership of their music. Fans get free access to discover independent talent without gatekeepers.

No pressure. No contracts. Just music — truly yours.

Why We Exist

Too many platforms take control away from creators. Truly Yours DSP exists to flip that model and build a home for artists.
